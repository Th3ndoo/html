Dogs

Highly intelligent, dogs have the capability of expressing their joy and happiness by wagging their tails. They are known to be the most loyal of animals. Dogs can sense your pain and can be your best friend. They can sense emotions, and when you are sad, they become sad and when you are happy, they are happy.

When livestock were domesticated about 7,000 to 9,000 years ago, dogs served as herders and guardians of sheep, goats, and cattle. Although many still serve in these capacities, dogs are mainly used for social purposes and companionship. Today dogs are employed as guides for the blind and disabled or for police work.5 days ago
